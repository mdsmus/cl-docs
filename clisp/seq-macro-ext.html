17.1.1. Macro EXT:DOSEQ

17.1.1. Macro EXT:DOSEQ

For iteration through a sequence, a macro EXT:DOSEQ, similar to DOLIST, may be used instead of MAP:

(EXT:DOSEQ (variable seqform [resultform])
  {declaration}*
  {tag|form}*)

EXT:DOSEQ forms are iteration forms.
